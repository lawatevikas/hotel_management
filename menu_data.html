pohaa

Pohaa, also known as pauwa, sira, chira, or aval, among many other names, is flattened rice originating from the Indian subcontinent.

Idili

Idli or idly are a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Southern India and in Sri Lanka.

Medu vada

Medu vada is a South Indian breakfast snack made from Vigna mungo. It is usually made in a doughnut shape

bred upma

Bread upma is a South Indian breakfast made with bread, onions, tomatoes, spices, nuts and herbs
